Title: Conversion Funnel Visualization

Prompt: Funnel chart for sales pipeline. Five stages: Visitors 10000, Signups 2500, Trials 800, Paid 200, Retained 150. Each stage different color getting darker. Show percentage drop between stages. Hover shows stage name, count, and conversion rate from previous. Display total conversion (1.5% visitor to retained) at bottom. Click stage to see detailed breakdown modal with metrics. Make it look slick with gradients.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, recharts, lucide-react, framer-motion
