Title: Budget Breakdown Tree Map

Prompt: Show me a budget breakdown as nested rectangles for three departments, each with a couple subcategories inside. Make each rectangle's size match its budget amount, with each rectangle having a subtle 3D depth effect showing raised surfaces. Use darker colors for departments and lighter shades for their subcategories. Flag any subcategory taking up too much of its parent's budget by coloring it red with a pulsing glow animation. Draw thin connecting lines from each subcategory rectangle to a breakdown panel positioned directly below the tree map showing how each subcategory breaks down as a percentage of its department. When I hover over a rectangle, animate the border to thicken gradually and show the amount plus percentage of total. Position a legend exactly below the breakdown panel explaining the color scheme. Load it with some realistic sample budget data.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react

Percentage calculations worked (C15 passed) and the breakdown panel showed data correctly (C16 passed). The legend appeared (C18 passed).
