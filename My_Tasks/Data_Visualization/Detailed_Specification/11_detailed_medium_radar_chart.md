Title: Multi-Axis Radar Chart

Prompt: Create a radar chart comparing three players' gaming stats across six attributes: Speed, Strength, Defense, Accuracy, Stamina, Strategy. Each axis ranges 0-100. Three overlapping polygons in different colors with semi-transparency. Display axis labels at each point. Show player names in legend with toggle to hide/show. Hover on polygon shows player name and all stats. Include average line showing balanced stats. Display radar chart title.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, recharts, lucide-react
