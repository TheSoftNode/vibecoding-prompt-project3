Title: Heatmap Calendar

Prompt: Build a calendar heatmap showing daily activity over 12 months. Grid with 12 rows (months) and 31 columns (days). Each cell colored by activity level: white (0), light blue (1-5), medium blue (6-10), dark blue (11+). Hover shows date and count. Month labels on left. Include color scale legend. Filter by activity threshold using slider. Display total activities count. Show month with highest activity in gold border.
