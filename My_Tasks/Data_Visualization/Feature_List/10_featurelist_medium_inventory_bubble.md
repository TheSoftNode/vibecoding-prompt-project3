Title: Inventory Distribution Bubble Chart

Prompt: Create an inventory bubble chart plotting products by stock level (x-axis) and sales velocity (y-axis). Each bubble's area represents product value with 3D sphere shading to show depth. Color bubbles by risk level based on stock-to-velocity ratio - red with pulsing glow for critical, yellow with pulsing glow for warning, green for healthy. Display product names inside bubbles. When clicking a bubble, animate it with a bouncing effect and draw thin curved lines from the bubble to a detail panel positioned directly below the chart showing name, stock, velocity, value, ratio in months, and risk category. Position a legend exactly below the detail panel explaining risk colors. Position bubbles without overlaps. Preload with sample product inventory data.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, recharts

## Justification

The Inventory Distribution Bubble Chart achieved 43% pass rate (9/21 criteria) with cascading failures from 3D effects, animations, and positioning. The chart displays 10 bubbles for Widgets A through J (C1 passed) positioned accurately on the x-axis by stock level (C2 passed) and y-axis by velocity (C3 passed). However, the bubble sizing completely failed C4 - instead of making area proportional to dollar value, the model made diameter proportional, causing Widget E ($30K) to appear 4× larger in area than it should compared to Widget G ($5K) rather than 6× larger, which caused C13 to fail automatically since overlap detection relies on accurate bubble sizes - Widgets C and E overlapped significantly. The model failed C5 catastrophically by rendering all bubbles as flat 2D circles instead of applying 3D sphere shading showing depth, losing the solid sphere effect. Product names displayed inside bubbles (C6 passed). The stock-to-velocity ratio calculation worked correctly (C7 passed). The color coding worked: Widget E appeared red for critical (C8 passed), but C9 failed - red bubbles displayed as solid color without pulsing glow animation. Widget A appeared yellow for warning (C10 passed), but C11 failed - yellow bubbles displayed without pulsing glow animation. Widget G appeared green for healthy (C12 passed). Click interactions worked: clicking a bubble (C14 passed), but the model failed to animate with bouncing effect - bubbles just showed a static highlight instead. The model failed C15 by drawing no curved lines from the clicked bubble to the detail panel, breaking visual correspondence. The detail panel appeared but failed C16 - it was positioned to the right of the chart instead of directly below it, causing C20 to fail in cascade since the legend couldn't be "exactly below the detail panel" when the panel wasn't below the chart. The detail panel showed name, stock, velocity, and value correctly (C17 passed). The ratio displayed correctly with one decimal place (C18 passed) and the risk category showed appropriately (C19 passed). The legend appeared (C21 passed).
