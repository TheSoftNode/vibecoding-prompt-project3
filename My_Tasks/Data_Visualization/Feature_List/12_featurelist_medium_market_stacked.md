Title: Market Share Evolution Stacked Area Chart

Prompt: Display a stacked area chart with 3D layered depth effects showing market share evolution for four companies over quarters. Stack areas to 100% at each quarter with smooth transitions and animated gradient flows moving left to right. Show companies stacked bottom to top in consistent order. When hovering an area, animate it with a pulsing effect and gray others with a tooltip. Provide filter buttons for full period vs recent half - redrawing for recent recalculates trend direction. Draw thin dotted lines from each company's area to a trend panel positioned directly below the chart displaying trend badges (Growing/Shrinking/Stable) next to company names that update when filter changes. Position a legend exactly below the trend panel explaining company colors and trend meanings. Preload with sample market share data showing realistic competitive dynamics.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, recharts

## Justification

The Market Share Evolution Stacked Area Chart achieved 41% pass rate (11/27 criteria) with cascading failures from 3D effects, animations, and positioning. The chart displays four company areas (C1 passed), but failed C2 catastrophically by rendering all areas as flat 2D shapes instead of applying 3D layered depth effects, losing the visual stacked-in-space hierarchy. Areas labeled A, B, C, D (C3 passed) across 12 quarters Q1-Q12 (C5 passed). However, the stacking order failed C4 - instead of A at bottom, B second, C third, D top, the areas appeared in reverse order with D at bottom and A at top. The 100% sum constraint failed C6 catastrophically - at Q6, the areas summed to 107%, creating a visible gap above the chart, indicating the model treated each company's percentage as independent rather than ensuring they summed to exactly 100% at each point. The start/end values worked partially: Company A correctly showed 45% in Q1 and 38% in Q12 (C7 passed), and Company B showed 30% to 35% (C8 passed), but Company C failed C9 by showing 15% to 18% instead of 15% to 20%, and Company D failed C10 by showing 10% to 9% instead of 10% to 7%. The curves appeared smooth rather than segmented (C11 passed), but C12 failed - areas displayed as static gradients instead of animating with gradient flows moving left to right. Hover interactions worked: the hovered area highlighted with increased opacity (C13 passed), but C14 failed - the hovered area displayed static highlighting instead of animating with pulsing effect. Other areas grayed out (C15 passed), and a tooltip displayed company name and percentage (C16 passed). The filter buttons displayed (C17 passed), but the redraw logic failed C18 - selecting "Recent Half" kept all 12 quarters visible and simply highlighted Q7-Q12 in a different shade instead of redrawing to show only those 6 quarters. The trend calculation failed C19 critically - it always calculated from Q1 to Q12 regardless of filter selection. The model failed C20 by drawing no dotted lines from company areas to the trend panel. The trend panel appeared but failed C21 - it was positioned to the right of the chart instead of directly below it, causing C26 to fail in cascade since the legend couldn't be "exactly below the trend panel" when the panel wasn't below the chart. The badge displays worked correctly showing "Growing", "Shrinking", or "Stable" based on the >2 point thresholds (C22, C23, C24 passed), but failed C25 by not updating when the filter changed, remaining stuck on Full Period calculations. Legend appeared (C27 passed).
