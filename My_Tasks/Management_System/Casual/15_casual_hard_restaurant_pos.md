Title: Restaurant POS System

Prompt: Full restaurant point-of-sale. Table layout shows tables numbered with status (Available, Occupied, Reserved). Click table to take order. Menu organized by categories. Add items to order with quantity and special instructions. Modifiers: size, add-ons, cooking preference. Order summary shows items, subtotal, tax, tip percentage selector, total. Split bill between multiple payments. Payment methods: cash, card. Kitchen display system shows new orders. Mark items as preparing/ready. Server alerts when order ready. Order history per table. Table turnover time tracking. Daily sales report: total sales, items sold, popular items chart. Inventory integration: reduce stock when item sold. Staff management: clock in/out, shift reports. Reservation system for tables with time slot. Waitlist for walk-ins. Make it fast and intuitive for busy restaurants. Pre-load with 12 tables, 30 menu items.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, date-fns, recharts, framer-motion
