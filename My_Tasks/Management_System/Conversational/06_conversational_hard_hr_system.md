Category: Management System

Prompt style: Conversational

Title: HR Employee Management System

Prompt: I need a complete HR system. Employee directory with profiles: photo, name, position, department, hire date, salary, manager, contact info. Org chart visualizes reporting structure. Leave management: employees request time off with dates and reason, managers approve/reject. Leave balance tracker shows used/remaining days per year. Performance reviews: create review with rating (1-5), comments, goals. Review history per employee. Department budget tracking shows headcount and total salaries. Payroll calculator computes monthly pay based on salary and deductions. Document storage per employee (upload/download files as base64). Birthday alerts for this month. Onboarding checklist for new hires. Dashboard: total employees, open positions, pending leave requests, upcoming birthdays, budget utilization chart. Pre-load 15 employees across 4 departments.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, date-fns, recharts, react-organizational-chart
