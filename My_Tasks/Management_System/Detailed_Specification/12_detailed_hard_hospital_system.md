Prompt style: Detailed Specification

Title: Hospital Patient Management

Prompt: Create hospital management system. Patient records: ID, name, age, blood type, allergies, emergency contact, medical history. Appointment scheduler: calendar view with doctor availability. Book appointment with patient, doctor, date/time, reason. Check-in marks patient arrived. Doctor can add diagnosis, prescriptions, notes. Prescription includes medicine name, dosage, duration. Billing system: procedures with costs, insurance coverage, patient balance. Payment recording reduces balance. Ward management: bed assignments, occupancy status. Lab test orders: create test request, enter results when ready. Results attached to patient record. Doctor schedule: shift timings, leave days. Patient queue shows waiting patients. Metrics dashboard: total patients, today's appointments, occupied beds percentage, pending lab results, outstanding payments total. Search patients by name or ID. Patient history timeline shows all visits chronologically. Emergency alert button flags critical patients in red. Pre-load with 20 patients, 5 doctors, 10 beds.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, date-fns, recharts
