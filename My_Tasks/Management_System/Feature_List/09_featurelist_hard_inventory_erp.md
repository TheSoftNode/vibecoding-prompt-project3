Prompt style: Feature List

Title: Full Inventory ERP System

Prompt: Build comprehensive inventory system. Products with SKU, name, category, quantity, reorder level, supplier, cost, selling price, location. Warehouse locations: multi-location tracking. Stock movements: receive stock, transfer between locations, adjust inventory. Purchase orders: create PO with supplier, products, quantities, expected date. Mark PO as received updates stock. Sales orders: create order, allocates stock, generates packing slip. Stock reservation when order placed. Low stock alerts when below reorder level. Supplier management: contact info, products supplied. Reporting: stock valuation, movement history, inventory turnover rate, profit margins. Barcode scanning simulation (input field). Batch/lot tracking. Expiry date warnings. Cycle counting for verification. Dashboard: stock value, low stock items, pending orders, top selling products chart. Export reports to CSV. Pre-load with 50 products, 3 warehouses, 5 suppliers.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, date-fns, recharts, papaparse
