Title: Mortgage Calculator

Prompt: I need a mortgage payment calculator. Inputs: loan amount, interest rate (annual %), loan term in years, down payment. Calculate button shows: monthly payment, total paid over life, total interest paid. Amortization table shows payment breakdown per year: principal vs interest. Extra payment field shows how much faster loan pays off. Chart visualizes principal vs interest over time. Property tax and insurance inputs add to monthly payment. Compare mode shows side-by-side for different scenarios. Print summary button.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, recharts, lucide-react
