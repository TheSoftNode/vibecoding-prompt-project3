Title: Bill Splitter Calculator

Prompt: Bill splitter for groups. Add people by name. Input total bill amount. Tip percentage dropdown (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, custom). Service charge toggle adds 10%. Display: subtotal, tip amount, service charge, grand total, per person amount. Uneven split mode: assign custom amounts per person. Shows how much each owes. Payment tracker: mark people as paid with checkmark. Remaining balance updates. Export split summary as text. Pre-load with 4 people for sample calculation.

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react
