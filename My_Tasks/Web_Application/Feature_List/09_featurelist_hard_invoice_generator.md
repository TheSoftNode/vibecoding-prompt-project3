Title: Invoice Generator with Tiered Discounts

Prompt: Build an invoice generator with three sections: Client Details, Line Items, and Invoice Summary. Client Details has fields for client name, email, and address. Line Items section allows adding items with description, quantity, unit price, and tax rate dropdown (0%, 5%, 10%, 15%). Each line item displays calculated subtotal (quantity × unit price) and tax amount. Display an Add Item button and Delete button for each item. Invoice Summary section shows: Subtotal (sum of all item subtotals before tax), Total Tax (sum of all item taxes), tiered discount if applicable (10% off when subtotal exceeds $500, 15% off when subtotal exceeds $1000, higher discount applies), Savings amount when discount active, and Grand Total (subtotal + tax - discount). All monetary calculations update immediately when quantities, prices, or tax rates change. Add payment terms dropdown (Net 30, Net 60, Net 90) that calculates due date from today's date. Display due date prominently. Show due date in red text with "Overdue Risk" warning if due date exceeds 45 days from today. Include a Generate PDF button that creates invoice preview showing all details. On initial load, display two sample line items: "Web Design" quantity 10 hours, $50/hour, 10% tax and "Hosting" quantity 1 year, $120/year, 5% tax. Tax rate for each item should calculate: item tax = (quantity × unit price) × (tax rate / 100).

Required libraries: react, tailwindcss, lucide-react, date-fns, jspdf
